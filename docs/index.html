FAQ

Is my data really private?

Yes. SafeCycle has zero network permissions. Your data never leaves your device. We literally cannot see your data even if we wanted to.

What is Stealth Mode?

Stealth Mode shows a fake calculator when someone enters your stealth PIN instead of your real PIN. This protects your privacy if someone forces you to unlock your phone.

How do I backup my data?

Go to Settings > Export Backup to create an encrypted backup file. Save it to a secure location.

What if I forget my PIN?

Unfortunately, there is no PIN recovery. You will need to delete and reinstall the app. This is by design for security.
