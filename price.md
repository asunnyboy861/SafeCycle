# Price Configuration

## Monetization Model
Paid Download (One-time Purchase)

## App Store Pricing
- **Price**: $7.99 (USD)
- **Setting Location**: App Store Connect → Your App → Pricing and Availability

## App Store Connect Setup Instructions
1. Go to App Store Connect → Your App → Pricing and Availability
2. Click "Add Pricing" or edit existing pricing
3. Select price tier corresponding to $7.99
4. Save

## Implementation Notes
- NO IAP configuration required
- NO StoreKit configuration required
- NO PurchaseManager required
- All features unlocked after download
- Price displayed on App Store product page
- No subscription, no recurring charges, no in-app purchases
